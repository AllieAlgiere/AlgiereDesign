About Me

Hello! I am Alex Algiere, a passionate web designer with sixteen years of personal experience specializing in front-end technologies such as HTML5, CSS3, and jQuery. My journey in web design has been driven by a love for creating visually appealing and user-friendly websites that provide exceptional user experiences.

I am a full-time student pursuing an Associate Degree in Applied Science with a focus on Web and UX Design. This academic pursuit enhances my skills and knowledge, preparing me for a professional web design and user experience career. While my expertise lies predominantly in front-end development, I continuously strive to learn and integrate new technologies and best practices in my projects.

I have a strong foundation in modern web design principles, including responsive design, UI/UX design, and cross-browser compatibility. Over the years, I have developed numerous personal projects, each reflecting my commitment to quality and innovation.

As I progress in my studies, I am eager to apply my skills and knowledge through internships and collaborative projects. I am actively seeking opportunities to work with experienced professionals, where I can contribute to exciting web design initiatives and further develop my expertise.

If you are looking for a dedicated and creative web designer for internships or collaborative projects, I would love to connect and discuss how we can work together.
