About Me

Hey there! I'm Alex, a dedicated web designer and developer with a passion for crafting captivating user experiences. Beyond coding with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I'm an avid writer and gaming enthusiast. Writing allows me to explore new worlds and ideas, while gaming fuels my creativity and strategic thinking.

My journey in web design began at fourteen, following a hip injury that led me to discover coding on a whim. I quickly became obsessed with it, particularly drawn to the creativity and structure of HTML5 and CSS3. These languages remain my favorites to this day, allowing me to blend aesthetics with functionality seamlessly.

I specialize in creating responsive and intuitive landing pages and interfaces that not only look great but also enhance user interaction. Whether it's optimizing user flows or refining design elements, I thrive on bringing ideas to life through thoughtful design and development.

Let's collaborate and create something extraordinary together!
